# Scribario Brand Guidelines

## Colors (Dark Theme)

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Background | #050505 | Primary background (near-black) |
| Background Alt | #0d0d0d | Alternating sections |
| Text Primary | #f5f5f5 | Headlines, body text |
| Text Secondary | #737373 | Muted labels, descriptions |
| Accent (Green) | #00E599 | CTAs, "live" indicators, success states — green = go energy |
| Accent Secondary (Blue) | #2AABEE | Telegram brand blue — chat elements, links |
| Accent Tertiary | #00B377 | Green hover/active states |
| Code Background | #111111 | Code blocks, terminal elements |
| Card Surface | #141414 | Cards, elevated surfaces |
| Card Surface Alt | #1a1a1a | Alternate card surfaces |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display/Headings | Clash Display | 700 (Bold), 600 (Semibold) | Hero: 6rem+, tight line-height (0.9) |
| Body | Cabinet Grotesk | 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium) | Clean, modern, humanist grotesk |
| Monospace | JetBrains Mono | 400 | Stats, code, commands |

All fonts self-hosted as WOFF2, `font-display: swap`, Latin subset only.

## Voice

**Three words:** Confident. Effortless. Direct.

- Short punchy sentences. Contractions always.
- Speaks like a friend who's really good at marketing
- No corporate speak, no hype
- **Avoid:** "AI-powered", "cutting-edge", "seamless", "unlock", "revolutionize", "leverage"

## Design Philosophy

1. **The product IS the design** — animated Telegram conversations are the hero
2. **Typography as architecture** — size, weight, color create structure, not cards
3. **Dark confidence** — dark bg + bright green/blue accents, high contrast
4. **Motion with purpose** — every animation demonstrates the product, nothing decorative

## What Scribario Is

AI social media automation that runs entirely through Telegram. No dashboard, no web app. You text what you want to post, AI generates 3 caption+image options, you tap approve, it posts everywhere.

**Target audience:** Small business owners (restaurants, retail, services) who hate managing social media.

**CTA:** "Get early access →" (no pricing, no "free trial" language)
**Bot link:** https://t.me/ScribarioBot
