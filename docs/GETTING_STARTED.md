# Getting Started with Scribario

Welcome. This is everything you need to know to run your social media on autopilot using @ScribarioBot.

---

## What Scribario Does

You send one text message. Scribario generates three unique caption + image options tuned to your brand voice, you tap to approve the one you like, and it posts automatically to your connected platforms. That's it.

No apps. No design skills. No copywriting. No scheduling tools. Just text your bot.

---

## Step 1 — Find Your Bot

Open Telegram and search for **@ScribarioBot**, or use the link from your welcome email.

Send `/start` to begin. The bot will walk you through a 2-minute brand setup. You only do this once.

---

## Step 2 — Connect Your Social Accounts

After setup, type `/connect` to link your social platforms.

**Currently supported:**
- Facebook (Pages)
- Instagram (Business accounts)
- LinkedIn, Bluesky, TikTok — coming soon

You must be an admin of any Facebook Page or Instagram account you want to connect. The bot will walk you through authorization step by step.

---

## Step 3 — Send Your First Request

Once connected, just describe what you want to post in plain English. No commands, no special format — just talk to it like a person.

**Examples that work great:**

> "Post about our weekend special"

> "We just hit 1,000 customers — celebrate it"

> "It's Monday. Post something motivational for business owners"

> "We're hiring a part-time assistant. Make it feel approachable"

> "National Coffee Day — make something fun and on-brand"

Scribario will confirm your request and generate content in about 30–60 seconds.

---

## Step 4 — Review Your 3 Options

When ready, you'll receive 3 options — each with a unique image and caption. They take different angles on the same request: one might be bold and punchy, one story-driven, one product-forward. You pick what fits.

**Your choices:**

| Button | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Approve #1 / #2 / #3** | That caption + image posts immediately to all connected platforms |
| **✏️ Edit #1 / #2 / #3** | Edit the caption before posting (see below) |
| **Reject All** | Nothing posts. Send a new request anytime |
| **Regenerate** | Generates 3 brand new options from your original request |

After approving, you'll receive a confirmation: **✅ Posted to: Facebook, Instagram**

---

## Editing a Caption Before You Post

Don't love the copy but like the image? Tap **✏️ Edit #2** (or whichever option you want).

The bot shows you the current caption and asks for your version. Type your edited caption and send it. The preview will refresh with your new copy — then tap Approve to post.

---

## Targeting Specific Platforms

By default, approved posts go to all your connected platforms. To post to just one or two, say it in your message:

> "Post this on Facebook only"

> "LinkedIn and Instagram only"

> "Only post to Facebook"

Scribario recognizes: **Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok**

---

## Scheduling Posts

To schedule a post instead of publishing immediately, include the date and time in your message:

> "Post about our weekend special on Saturday at 10am"

> "Schedule something for Monday morning at 9"

> "Post this Friday at noon"

Scribario will confirm the scheduled time. The post will generate now and go live at the time you specified.

---

## Choosing an Image Style

Include a style keyword in your message to control how images look:

| Say this | Image style |
|---|---|
| `cinematic` or `dramatic` | Movie-quality lighting, high drama |
| `cartoon` or `illustrated` or `animated` | Artistic, illustrated look |
| `watercolor` or `painted` | Soft, hand-painted feel |
| `photorealistic` or `realistic` | Clean, professional photography |

**Example:**

> "Post about our new product launch. Make it cinematic."

> "Something fun about our team. Illustrated style."

If you don't specify, Scribario uses your brand's default style (set during onboarding).

---

## Sending a Reference Photo

Want Scribario to match a specific look, color, or subject? Send a photo to the bot before your request.

The AI will use your photo as creative direction — matching the lighting, tone, or composition in the generated images. Great for keeping your visual identity consistent.

Just send the photo, then follow it with your content request.

---

## Viewing Your Post History

Type `/history` to see your last 10 approved posts — with the date, platforms, and a caption preview for each.

---

## Tips for Better Results

**Be specific:**
> "Post about our new hot sauce — it's sweet upfront with serious heat at the end. Make it feel adventurous."

Not:
> "Post about our product."

**Include the emotion:**
> "This should feel exciting — we're celebrating a milestone."

**Tell it what NOT to do:**
> "Don't make it salesy — keep it genuine and community-focused."

**Give context about your audience:**
> "This is for our VIP customers who've been with us since day one."

**Combine features:**
> "Post something cinematic about our weekend special on Facebook only, schedule for Saturday at 11am"

## FAQ

**How long does generation take?**
Usually 30–60 seconds. Images are generated in parallel so the wait is mostly image time.

**What if none of the 3 options are right?**
Tap Regenerate for 3 new options, or reject and send a new request with more specific direction.

**Can I post to multiple platforms at once?**
Yes — connect multiple accounts and all will receive the post when you approve. Or specify which platforms in your message.

**How does the brand voice get better over time?**
Every caption you approve gets saved as a style example. Future generations use your approved posts as reference — the longer you use Scribario, the more it sounds like you.

**Can I add team members?**
Yes. Contact support to add additional Telegram accounts to your business.

**Is my content saved?**
All requests, drafts, and approved posts are saved to your account. Use `/history` to view recent posts.

**What if the image isn't right?**
Tap Regenerate for completely new options, or add style keywords to your next request to guide the image direction.

**I approved a post — when does it go live?**
Immediately, unless you scheduled it. You'll receive a confirmation message in Telegram once it's live.
