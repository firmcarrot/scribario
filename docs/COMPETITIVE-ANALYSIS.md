## Scribario's Unique Position

**What NO competitor does today:**

1. **Conversational interface** — Telegram chat, not a web dashboard. Zero learning curve.
2. **Real AI-generated images** — Diffusion model (Nano Banana 2), not templates or stock photos.
3. **AI video generation** — Short + long video from text (Veo 3.1). Nobody in this price range offers this.
4. **Brand voice learning** — Few-shot examples from approved posts. Gets better over time.
5. **One-tap approval** — Human stays in the loop but does minimal work.
6. **True auto-posting** — Not just scheduling. Generates AND posts.
7. **Price point** — $29-99/mo for full service vs $750+ for a human.

**The gap:** No competitor combines conversational AI + real image generation + video + brand voice + auto-posting at the SMB price point ($29-99/mo).

## Pricing Model Analysis

### Industry Trends (2025-2026)

| Model | Adoption | Trend |
|---|---|---|
| **Hybrid (subscription + usage)** | 61% of SaaS | Growing — best of both worlds |
| **Pure subscription (flat rate)** | 28% of SaaS | Declining — users want flexibility |
| **Pure usage/credits** | 11% of SaaS | Niche — causes "credit anxiety" in non-technical users |

**Source:** OpenView Partners SaaS Pricing Benchmarks 2025

### What Works for SMBs

1. **"Posts" not "credits"** — SMB owners think in posts per month, not tokens or credits. "30 posts/mo" is instantly understood. "150 credits" requires math.

2. **Predictable costs** — SMBs budget monthly. Surprise overages cause churn. Flat or capped pricing wins.

3. **Visible value** — Each tier should clearly say what you get. "30 image posts + 5 videos" beats "150 credits (varies by usage)."

4. **Low barrier to entry** — Free trial or very cheap starter tier. SMBs won't commit $99/mo without trying first.

5. **No punishment for success** — If a post goes viral and they want to post more, don't make them feel penalized with overage charges.

### Credit Anxiety Problem

Pure credit/token models cause measurable issues with non-technical SMB users:
- **Hesitation to use the product** — "I don't want to waste credits"
- **Confusion about value** — "How many credits is a post? What about a video?"
- **Surprise depletion** — User runs out mid-month, feels ripped off
- **Comparison shopping** — Credits invite per-unit comparison with competitors

**Recommendation:** Sell in "posts" with clear per-tier limits. Credits can exist internally for metering but should not be user-facing.

## Recommended Pricing Structure

### Three Tiers (Post-Based, Not Credit-Based)

| Tier | Price/mo | Image Posts | Short Videos | Long Videos | Platforms | Content Library |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $29/mo | 15 | 3 | 0 | 3 platforms | 25 slots |
| **Growth** | $59/mo | 40 | 10 | 2 | 6 platforms | 100 slots |
| **Pro** | $99/mo | 100 | 25 | 5 | 9 platforms (all) | Unlimited |

### Content Library

Every generation gives 3 options. User approves 1. The other 2 can be **saved to Content Library** via a "Save to Library" button. Saved items can be posted later for free. Rules:
- **Post from library as-is** → free (doesn't count against monthly limit)
- **AI-assisted edit on library item** → counts as 1 post
- **Manual text edit (user types it)** → free

### Our Cost Per Tier (Worst Case — 100% Utilization, CORRECTED 2026-03-13)

| Tier | Image Post Cost | Short Video Cost | Long Video Cost | Total COGS | Margin |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | 15 x $0.16 = $2.40 | 3 x $0.48 = $1.44 | 0 | $3.84 | 87% |
| **Growth** | 40 x $0.16 = $6.40 | 10 x $0.48 = $4.80 | 2 x $2.18 = $4.36 | $15.56 | 74% |
| **Pro** | 100 x $0.16 = $16.00 | 25 x $0.48 = $12.00 | 5 x $2.18 = $10.90 | $38.90 | 61% |

**Even at 100% utilization on Pro, margin is 61%.** In practice, most users use 40-60% of their allocation (industry standard), pushing real margins to 80%+.

### Why This Structure

1. **$29 Starter** — Undercuts Alkai.ai ($27 Pro), includes image generation they don't have. Low enough for any SMB to try.
2. **$59 Growth** — Sweet spot for active businesses posting 3-5x/week. Video unlocked here.
3. **$99 Pro** — Power users, agencies managing clients. All platforms, generous limits. Still 10x cheaper than a freelancer.
4. **No free tier** — Free users don't convert and consume support. 7-day free trial instead (with full Growth features).

### Add-On Packs (Revenue Expansion)

| Add-On | Price | What You Get |
|---|---|---|
| Extra 10 image posts | $5 | For users who hit their limit |
| Extra 5 short videos | $12 | Video-heavy users |
| Extra 1 long video | $6 | One-off video needs |
| Brand voice setup | $29 one-time | White-glove AI brand profile from website scrape + call |

## Threat Assessment

### High Threat
- **Alkai.ai** — Same target customer, similar price point, fully automated. Watch closely. Their weakness: no approval step, no video, generic images.

### Medium Threat
- **Predis.ai** — If they add a conversational interface and better image gen, they'd be competitive. Currently too dashboard-heavy for the "I just want my phone to do it" SMB.
- **Meta AI tools** — Meta is building AI content creation into Facebook/Instagram business tools. If they nail it, it's free and native. Timeline unknown.

### Low Threat
- **Buffer/Hootsuite/Sprout** — Different market (tools for people who already create content). They're not trying to replace the content creator.
- **Jasper/Copy.ai** — Text only. No images, no posting. Different product category.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

1. **Launch at $29/$59/$99** — Simple, competitive, high-margin. Don't overthink pricing before product-market fit.

2. **7-day free trial (Growth tier)** — Let users experience the full value. No credit card required. Convert with the "your trial is ending" nudge.

3. **Video is the moat** — No competitor at this price point does AI video. Lead with it in marketing. "AI-generated video posts for $59/mo" is a headline.

4. **"Posts" not "credits"** — User-facing unit is always "posts." Internal metering can use whatever system you want.

5. **Watch Alkai.ai** — They're the closest threat. If they add video or improve image quality, differentiate on brand voice accuracy and approval workflow.

6. **Don't compete on price** — Compete on value vs. hiring a person. "$59/mo vs $2,000/mo for a social media manager" is the real comparison.

7. **Annual discount** — Offer 20% off for annual billing ($279/$567/$948/yr). Reduces churn, improves cash flow.
