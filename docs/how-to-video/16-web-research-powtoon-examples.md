## 8-Part Prompt Framework

1. Scene description
2. Visual style definition
3. Camera movement specification
4. Main subject detail
5. Background/setting
6. Lighting and mood
7. Audio cues
8. Color palette guidance

## Full Example Prompts

### Character-Driven Storytelling
"A weathered sea captain with thick grey beard and blue knitted hat stands at ship's railing, gesturing toward stormy ocean waves. Cinematic close-up with slow dolly-in on weathered face. Golden hour lighting with dramatic shadows. He says: 'The ocean teaches you respect, one wave at a time.' Audio: ocean waves crashing, wind, no background music. Color palette: deep blues, warm amber, weathered browns. (no subtitles)"

### Product Showcase
"Premium smartwatch rotates elegantly on glass surface in minimalist studio. 360-degree camera movement revealing all angles. Clean, bright lighting with subtle reflections. Professional female voice-over highlights key features. Audio: soft corporate music, clear narration. Color palette: pristine whites, metallic silver, accent blue. Modern tech aesthetic."

### Social Media (Fun/Viral)
"Golden retriever wearing sunglasses sits at café table with laptop, 'typing' with paws. Handheld camera capturing absurd scene naturally. Passersby react with amused glances. Upbeat, quirky background music. Audio: café ambience, keyboard clicking, cheerful indie music. Color palette: warm café browns, golden fur tones, vibrant street colors. Fun, lighthearted mood."

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Dull lighting | Add: "soft rim light," "studio key lighting," "dramatic shadows" |
| Camera misses focus | Lead with subject: "close-up of woman's face" |
| Unwanted elements | Replace negatives with positives: "open desert landscape" not "no walls" |
| Missing/odd audio | Be direct: "city ambience," "soft jazz," "no background music" |

## Character Consistency Template

"Sarah, woman in early 30s with shoulder-length auburn hair, wearing navy business suit and silver-rimmed glasses, with confident but approachable expression"

Copy this VERBATIM to every prompt for the same character.

## Genre-Specific Tones

- **Cinematic drama:** "cinematic lighting," "shallow DOF," warm palettes, slow camera
- **Corporate:** "clean lighting," "professional aesthetic," neutral colors, stable camera
- **Social/viral:** "handheld style," "natural lighting," vibrant colors, dynamic movements
- **Documentary:** "realistic lighting," "handheld camera," "natural environments"

## Style References

- "In the style of a Wes Anderson film with symmetrical framing"
- "Documentary-style with handheld camera and natural lighting"
- "Music video aesthetic with dynamic cuts and vibrant colors"
