# Veo 3 Prompt Guide — Google DeepMind (Official)

**Source:** https://deepmind.google/models/veo/prompt-guide/

## Core Principle

"The more detail you add, the more control you'll have over the final output."

## Key Compositional Elements

### Shot Framing and Motion
- Specify camera positioning (low-angle, high-angle, etc.)
- Define camera movement (pans, tracking)
- Experiment with different perspectives

### Style
- Declare visual format upfront: cartoon, claymation, film noir, VHS aesthetic
- Establishes overall visual texture and tone

### Lighting
- Describe light temperature (warm, cold)
- Specify distribution (even, spotlit)
- Include atmospheric effects

### Character Descriptions (SPECIFIC > VAGUE)
- GOOD: "A woman in her twenties with wavy brown hair and light freckles"
- Include appearance, voice characteristics, and actions

### Location (SENSORY LANGUAGE)
- GOOD: "smoky jazz club at night"
- BAD: "jazz club"
- Build atmosphere through descriptive specificity

### Action
- Describe with precision what characters are doing
- Include movement sequences and interactions
- Specify physical behaviors

### Dialogue
- Assign specific dialogue to characters
- Use dialogue to establish tone

## Prompt Strategy Categories

### Character-Focused
Medium shot + character description + specific action + dialogue piece

### World-Building
Use "evocative, sensory language" for environments:
- Light quality and color
- Texture and material details
- Atmospheric conditions
- Scale and inhabitation

### Complex Action Sequences
"Map out specific play-by-play sequences with nothing left to chance" — specify every vehicle movement, environmental interaction, visual effect

### Audio Integration
- Pair visual elements with matching audio
- Create multi-sensory experiences
- Integrate audio cues into prompts

### Narrative Construction
Build stories around simple objects/events:
- Clear beginning, middle, end
- Sense of purpose and adventure
- Narrative momentum

## Tools
- **Gemini** — use to expand and add detail to prompts
- **Flow** — dedicated Veo 3 generation interface
